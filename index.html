Getting Started!

Welcome to your Virtual Consultation! This form is designed to save you time, and it lets us get an idea of what treatment to recommend for you!

Since we tailor a treatment plan to fit each patients unique needs, we need to take a look at your bite.

Grab a friend and take the 7 pictures listed below to get started.

Once you’ve taken a clear picture of each required photo and filled out the necessary information, click the “Submit” button and leave the rest to us!

Give us a few days to review your info, and one of our team members will reach out to you using the contact information you provide.

One: Smiling Front

Look directly into the camera and smile a full, natural smile!

Two: Relaxed Front

Now relax your face and take a picture without smiling.

Three: Right Profile

Keep your face relaxed and turn to your left, so the camera captures the right side of your face.

Four: Left Bite

Clench your teeth and bite down on your molars. Pull your cheeks back as far as you can.

Five: Right Bite

Again, but on the right side, clench your teeth and bite down on your molars. Pull your cheeks back as far as you can.

Six: Upper

Tilt your head back, and position the camera so that the picture includes the roof of the mouth and all teeth.

Seven: Lower

Tilt the chin down slightly, and open your mouth wide. Pull the tongue back slightly, making sure all lower teeth can be seen in the picture.
